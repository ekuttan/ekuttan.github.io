Give Plasma, Save Life

Plasma from recovered COVID-19 patients is urgently needed across hospitals in India.

Dear Covid Warrior,

Congratulations on recovering from COVID-19 and thank you for showing interest in becoming a plasma donor!

You were fortunate enough to go home without any complications. But there are a few others who develop serious disease and need treatment with convalescent plasma therapy.

Even amongst COVID-19 survivors, only few qualify to become donors which makes plasma a difficult treatment resource to access. It is especially important to mobilize and consolidate a donor database across country and make plasma available to needy patients!

The reason you are back to good health is that your plasma now contains antibodies that helped you fight Coronavirus. Your plasma can now help treat people whose body is not able to fight the virus.

Tanvi & Nikhil
